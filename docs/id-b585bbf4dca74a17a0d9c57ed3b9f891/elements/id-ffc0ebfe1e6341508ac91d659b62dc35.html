7.5 Participation in eHealth Network expert bodies ()
Member States shall participate in expert bodies and communities under eHealth Network, such as eHealth Network Subgroup on Semantics and eHealth Network Technical Subgroup. Member States shall be encouraged to explicitly name representatives in the expert bodies and communities under eHealth Network.
